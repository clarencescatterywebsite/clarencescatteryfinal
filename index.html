💖 What’s in a Name?

🐱 Our Cattery is named after our handsome, gentle, caring boy Clarence. We sadly lost him in June 2016 at the very grand age of 24 years old. I was privileged to have had Clarence with me since he was 9 weeks old - rescued from a farm where he was neglected and mistreated.

😼 As a kitten, he was extremely mischievous — climbing curtains, sitting on rooftops, fighting with my pigtails and even stealing a joint of beef from my next door neighbour’s kitchen worktop!!

In his older years, he was a best friend to both of my children. They dressed him up, put make-up on him, talked to him, and most importantly, they loved him to pieces.

🐾 Clarence was so loving, so affectionate, and his love unconditional... we all miss him terribly but were honoured to have him in our lives.

About Me!

👋 Hello, my name is Kerry AKA Auntie Kes. I created Clarence’s as I wanted to make a special, unique place for pussycats to experience a wonderful holiday. Somewhere they get to feel safe, have lots of fun, be mischievous, relax, have cuddles galore, be pampered and thoroughly loved. I understand that it can be initially unsettling for your baby to adjust to its new surroundings, equally unsettling for the owners to leave their much-loved family member for the first time. I wanted to provide a second home for our feline friends — whilst here, they are absolutely part of my family.

🐾 I have had cats for as long as I can remember and would never be without one. I have been blessed to have Ginny, Tawney, Joecat, Clarence, Dennis, Carlito, Humphrey, Dotty and Ralph in my life. Only one remains — my big Teddy Bear, Ralphie...

🐾 Accommodation

🐱 Clarence’s Cattery was designed purely for the comfort, happiness and safety of each guest by myself, purchased from Pedigree Pens and built by my husband, David AKA Uncle Dave. The cattery is completely UPVC which is absolutely fantastic as a high level of cleanliness can be kept at all times. The roof is made from a heat reflective material which helps to keep it cool in the summer. It is located in my garden, very close to my home so even when I am not in there (which is hardly ever) I can see in and they can see me.

🐞 There are six suites, all built to the highest standards to comply with DEFRA regulations to obtain my FIVE star rating. We have the Ladybug, Bumble, Frog, Butterfly, Fish and Bird suites and each can accommodate two pussycats from the same household. For families of more than two, the suites can be opened up with the suite next door. Each suite has a bedroom which is fitted with a thermostatically controlled heater for the colder days, it is also insulated so stays lovely and warm in the winter. There is an iron bed for those pussycats who like to lounge and a pod for those who like to curl up, all furnished with fluffy blankets of course. Large litter trays, so plenty of space to move around when doing their business. Each suite also has an additional room which has a tower with scratching posts and hidey hole, a small chair for fluffy bottoms and a selection of toys. For those more adventurous climbers we have very high shelves too.

🦜 The front of the suite is pawsafe mesh which leads to a safety corridor and looks out onto my garden. There are strictly no communal areas, pussycats from different households are not allowed to mix to ensure everyone is safe, healthy and happy. The mesh in the top of the safety corridor allows a constant flow of fresh air for our guests to breathe in. The garden is full of plant pots which attract butterflies and Bee’s for the pussycats to watch. We even get a cheeky squirrel called Steve who makes many an appearance to keep them entertained and a Heron who loves to land with his big feet on the roof!

🎶 The tunes are also always playing in the corridor; Classic FM is a firm favourite, rather than Auntie’s singing !

📋 Other Requirements:

All cats MUST be fully vaccinated against: 🐾 FHV – Feline Herpesvirus - yearly
🐾 FCV – Feline Calicivirus - yearly
🐾 FPV – Feline Panleucopaenia Virus – every three years If they are not vaccinated against the above, then I'm afraid they cannot stay. If your cat misses a booster then they will need a restart.
Restarts require two injections 3–4 weeks apart and both injections must be completed at least two weeks prior to arrival.

All cats MUST be up-to-date with their flea and worm treatment before coming into Clarence’s Cattery. Details need to be provided.
